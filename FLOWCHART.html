📊 Key Components

  • Auto-Offset Learning: Compass automatically calibrates by comparing GPS course (when moving > 3 km/h) with magnetic heading
  • GPS Smoothing: Exponential Moving Average (EMA) with α=0.3 reduces noise
  • Navigation Loop: Runs at GPS update rate (~5Hz) with compass updates at 10Hz
  • Motor Control: Proportional steering when heading error < 25°, otherwise pure rotation
  • Waypoint Tracking: Advances to next waypoint when within 5 meters
